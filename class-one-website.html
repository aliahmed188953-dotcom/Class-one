Questions

Asked before. Answered here.

How fast can you arrange a car?

Often within the hour in and around Cologne, subject to availability. Pre-booked journeys can be arranged anywhere in Europe with notice.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

Every airport pickup is tracked live. Your chauffeur adjusts to the actual landing time — the meet & greet happens when you land, not when you were scheduled to.

Can you sign an NDA?

Yes — before any itinerary details are shared. Discretion is standard for every client; formal confidentiality agreements are available on request.

Do you handle multi-day tours and roadshows?

Yes. One coordinator plans the full itinerary, fleet and timing across cities, and remains your single point of contact throughout.

How does invoicing work for companies?

Corporate accounts receive one consolidated monthly invoice covering all journeys, with per-trip breakdowns for your records.
